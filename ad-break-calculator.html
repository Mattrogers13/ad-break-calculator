How to use Ad Break Calculator

Getting Started

  • ⏱Enter your show's total run time in Show Duration using HH:MM:SS;FF format. You can also type bare digits and it will auto-format on Tab or Enter. For example, typing 4512000 becomes 00:45:12;00.
  • ⚡Choose a Strategy and click Calculate Breaks:
    • •Redistribute discards all current positions and spaces every unlocked break evenly from scratch. example
    • •Snap keeps each break close to where it is but nudges it to the nearest position that satisfies the 6 to 10 minute spacing rule. example
    • •Preserve leaves every placed break exactly where it is and only fills in the empty slots around them. example

Working with Breaks

  • ✏️Click any timecode to edit it directly. Bare digit entry works here too.
  • 🔒Lock a break to pin it in place. Locked breaks are skipped during recalculation, so you can anchor one position and freely adjust everything else around it.
  • ⧉Copy a single timecode with the Copy button, or use Copy All to copy every break at once as a list ready to paste into your edit system.

Reading the Timeline

  • ■Green gap means spacing is valid (6 to 10 minutes). Click any marker to scroll to that break.
  • ■Red gap means spacing is too short (under 6 min), too long (over 10 min), or the break falls inside the 3-minute end buffer.
  • ■Yellow line marks the 3-minute end boundary. No break should be placed past this point.
